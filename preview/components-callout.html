Standard — Sage Advice

Sage Advice

The spell's damage increases when you reach higher levels. At 5th level, the melee attack deals an extra 1d6 fire damage to the target, and the fire damage to other creatures increases to 2d6. Both damage rolls increase by 1d6 at 11th and 17th level.

Arcane Lore — Emerald Variant

Arcane Focus

An arcane focus is a special item designed to channel the power of arcane spells. A sorcerer, warlock, or wizard can use such an item as a spellcasting focus in place of any material component which does not list a cost.

Legendary Action

The ancient dragon can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time, and only at the end of another creature's turn. The dragon regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
