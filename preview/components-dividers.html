Dividers & Ornamental Corners

Sigil dividers with seven unique SVG glyphs, double-rule and dashed rule breaks, and L-shaped ornamental corner marks for content cards.

Rule Breaks

Between major chapters of the compendium, a double gold rule announces the transition.

Double Rule border-bottom: 2px double var(--gold-deep)
Use between major content sections

Between entries within a section, a dashed ink rule marks the boundary.

Dashed Rule border-bottom: 1px dashed var(--ink-faded)
Use between list items, stat rows, card sections

Sigil Dividers — Seven Glyphs

I — Dragon Eye

The eye that watches all, seeing through illusion and shadow.

II — Star

The guiding stars by which all navigators chart their course.

III — Rune Circle

Ancient runes bound in a circle of perpetual warding.

IV — Crossed Quills

The scribe's mark: crossed quills, inked in iron-gall and bound with wax.

V — Shield

The shield of the realm, bearing the ward against all who would trespass.

VI — Ouroboros

The serpent consuming its tail — time unending, death rebirthed.

VII — Compass Rose

North, south, east, west — the cardinal paths of the known and unknown world.

Ornamental Corners

Ornamental corners mark the boundary of content cards using absolutely-positioned spans with L-shaped gold borders. They are decorative only (aria-hidden="true") and must not overlap readable text at 400% browser zoom.

Spec 4 × <span aria-hidden="true"> · 28×28px
border-color: --gold · border-style: solid
.tl: border-width 2px 0 0 2px (top-left L)
.tr: border-width 2px 2px 0 0
.bl: border-width 0 0 2px 2px
.br: border-width 0 2px 2px 0
Positioned: absolute · inset 8px from card edge
