Accent Palette

Gold, blood, emerald, sapphire — use sparingly. Blood and gold are reserved for elements that demand attention.

Gold Scale

--gold-bright #d4a548 Active sidebar text, highlights ⚠ Decorative text only
--gold #b8893b Borders, icons, dividers ⚠ Not for body text on parchment
--gold-deep #7a5a22 Text-safe gold. Double-rule borders. AA ✓ 5.4:1 on p-100

Blood Scale

--blood #7a1212 Primary actions, numerals, drop caps AA ✓ 9.4:1 on p-100
--blood-deep #4a0808 Hover states, btn shadows AA ✓ 14.2:1 on p-100

Magic School Accents

--emerald #2d5a3d Arcane / Conjuration school AA ✓ 7.2:1 on p-100
--sapphire #1e3a5f Divine / Divination school AA ✓ 9.8:1 on p-100

Usage Rules

✕Never use --emerald and --sapphire together on the same component.
✕Never use --gold or --gold-bright as essential text on parchment — use --gold-deep instead.
✕--blood and --gold are reserved for elements that demand attention — use sparingly, at most twice per screen.
