Sage Advice
"A creature can make a Strength check to break free of a grapple as an action. The DC equals the grappler's passive Athletics score (10 + Athletics bonus)."
Danger · Dragon's Warning

Arcane Note
"Concentration spells require the caster to maintain focus. Any time a caster takes damage, they must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC = 10 or half the damage taken, whichever is higher)."
